Motto


We believed that paying hefty brokerage cannot be the only option to find a new home. As tenants, we have been paying these brokerages year on year without seeing any broker’s advantage. The only reason he existed was that there was a huge information asymmetry in the market. RentToday is a platform that removes this information asymmetry and provides a marketplace for free exchange of this information that used to cost 1-2 months of rent as brokerage.
